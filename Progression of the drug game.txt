Possible progression for the drug game

You spawn
You can do a few misc tasks in town
You do a favor for a friend
You return to your mother, but she wants to know why you're being followed by cops. 
The cop tells you you must fight in the army (possibly a fantasy world)
Your mother rejects you.
There is a story campaign to follow, but you must acquire 1000 or 10000 gold to pay the Marshall. 
Once the Marshall is paid, you automatically get a pass to go home. You can stop playing the campaign.
Once you get home, you arrive either to a burned down home or a welcoming mother.
